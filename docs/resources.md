# Resources & References

## Qiskit / Tooling

- [IBM Quantum Learning](https://learning.quantum.ibm.com/) — Free structured courses, interactive textbook. Start here.
- [Qiskit Docs](https://docs.quantum.ibm.com/) — API reference, tutorials
- [Qiskit GitHub](https://github.com/Qiskit/qiskit) — Source & examples
- [Qiskit Algorithms](https://qiskit-community.github.io/qiskit-algorithms/) — VQE, QAOA, QPE, Grover implementations

## Textbooks

- **Nielsen & Chuang** — *Quantum Computation and Quantum Information* (Cambridge) — the standard reference
- **Preskill** — [Lecture Notes on Quantum Computation](http://www.theory.caltech.edu/~preskill/ph229/) — free online, excellent depth
- **Wilde** — *Quantum Information Theory* — rigorous, information-theoretic perspective
- **Barnett** — *Quantum Information* (Oxford) — more accessible, photon-friendly examples

## Quantum Algorithms

- Grover, L.K. (1996). *A fast quantum mechanical algorithm for database search*. arXiv:quant-ph/9605043
- Shor, P.W. (1994). *Algorithms for quantum computation*. FOCS.
- Farhi et al. (2014). *A Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm*. arXiv:1411.4028

## Quantum Optics / Photonic QIP (bridges our background)

- Kok et al. (2007). *Linear optical quantum computing with photonic qubits*. Rev. Mod. Phys. 79, 135
- Flamini et al. (2019). *Photonic quantum information processing: a review*. Rep. Prog. Phys. 82, 016001
- O'Brien (2007). *Optical quantum computing*. Science 318, 1567

## Open Quantum Systems (relevant to our non-Hermitian / photon blockade work)

- Breuer & Petruccione — *The Theory of Open Quantum Systems*
- Gardiner & Zoller — *Quantum Noise*
- Minganti et al. (2019). *Spectral theory of Liouvillians for dissipative phase transitions*. PRA 100, 062131
